BEIJING, Oct. 21  -- China's gross domestic product expanded 7.3 percent from a year ago in the third quarter of this year, the National Bureau of Statistics announced Tuesday.

Though the GDP growth in July-September marked the slowest quarterly growth since the first quarter of 2009, it was still within the reasonable range set by policymakers.
